# Enemy Redesign Requirements

Output directory: `art/enemies_redesign/`

Global rules:
- Based on the original enemy image as reference.
- Enemy faces left.
- In-game standing portrait, not poster art or concept cover.
- Clear silhouette > body structure > monster feature > material > local effect > decorative detail.
- Max one theme color, one main material, one core visual symbol, and one limited local effect.
- No heavy smoke, spirit tentacles, particle storms, floating shards, energy orbs, large rings, or effects hiding the body.
- Local effects only on eyes, weapon, joints, feet, wing edges, or tiny cracks.

Per-enemy single focus:
- `abyssal_sword_soul`: dark blue sword-shaped soul, cold metal, one crossed blade glow.
- `ash_imp`: ash-brown small imp, soot skin, ember eyes only.
- `black_lotus_matriarch`: black-purple lotus matriarch, cloth/petal body, one lotus chest mark.
- `blood_tiger`: dark red tiger, coarse fur, blood claw mark, red eyes only.
- `bone_dragon`: pale bone dragon, exposed rib spine, green eye only.
- `bronze_puppet`: bronze puppet, riveted bronze armor, round core joint glow.
- `bull_demon`: dark red horned bull demon, hide/iron, horns as core symbol, eye glow only.
- `dark_eagle`: dark feather eagle, sharp wing silhouette, faint wing-edge glow.
- `eclipse_tyrant`: blood moon, broken emperor, dark red metal, crown/eye glow only.
- `grave_lantern`: ghost lantern, old metal, blue-purple soul flame inside only.
- `iron_golem`: rusty heavy armor, giant fists, rusted iron, tiny joint glow.
- `jade_spider`: jade carapace spider, thin toxic silk, green fang/silk only.
- `jade_wyrm`: jade-scaled coiled wyrm, mist limited near mouth only.
- `mist_wolf`: shadow wolf body, purple-gray fur, cold eyes only.
- `paper_soldier`: paper talisman armor, torn straw hat, yellow-brown talisman glow only.
- `river_serpent`: teal river serpent, wet scales, single curved body, eye glint only.
- `shadow_reaper`: shadow robe reaper, scythe, violet eye/weapon edge only.
- `silver_moon_wolf`: silver wolf, crescent mark, cold eyes only.
- `sky_palace_guardian`: gold armor guardian, halberd, chest gem glow only.
- `stone_goblin`: moss stone body, heavy rocky silhouette, small moss patches only.
- `storm_hawk`: blue storm hawk, sharp wings, wing-edge lightning only.
- `thunder_roc`: blue thunder roc, broad wings, claw lightning only.
- `venom_moth`: green venom moth, powder wings, tiny wing dust only.
